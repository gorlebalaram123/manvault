How to Measure Your Foot

  1. Stand on a piece of paper with your heel against the wall
  2. Mark the longest toe and measure from heel to toe
  3. Measure both feet - use the larger measurement
  4. Add 0.2-0.4 inches for comfort in closed shoes
  5. Measure at the end of the day when feet are largest
Note: Shoe sizes may vary by brand. Check product descriptions for specific sizing.
